At Nuveli ("we", "us", "our"), we value the privacy of our users ("you", "your"). This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account info: Email, name (optional).
  • Profile data: Age, gender, height, weight, activity level, goal.
  • Meal records: Photos, food descriptions, calorie/nutrient values.
  • Health data: Water, weight, mood records.
  • Device data: Push token, device type, app version.
  • Usage: Screen views, button clicks (anonymous analytics).

2. Use of Data

  • To generate AI analysis and coach responses (sent to OpenAI API).
  • To create personalized progress charts and insights.
  • To send notifications.
  • To improve service quality and detect errors.

3. Third Parties

  • Supabase — Data storage and authentication.
  • OpenAI — Meal analysis and coach responses (images not stored).
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management.
  • Firebase — Push notifications, analytics, error reporting.

4. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Your Rights (GDPR + KVKK)

In-app, via "Settings > Privacy & Safety":

  • Download your data.
  • Delete your account and all data.
  • Manage notification and data sharing preferences.
  • Request to stop processing.

6. Security

Data is transmitted encrypted via TLS. Your health information is accessible only to you. Protected by Supabase Row Level Security (RLS).

7. Children's Privacy

Nuveli does not serve users under 13. Users under 18 require parental consent. We will close any account if we detect underage users.

8. Contact

Questions: support@nuveli.com.tr
