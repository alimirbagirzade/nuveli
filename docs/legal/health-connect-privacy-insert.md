# Health Connect — privacy-policy insert (7 languages)

The live policy at **nuveli.com.tr/privacy** (dated 2026-05-06) predates the
phone-health-import feature and does **not** mention Health Connect. Google's
Health Connect program **requires** the privacy policy to disclose the data it
reads, how it is used, and that it is not shared — otherwise the Play "Health
apps declaration" is rejected.

**Action:** add the matching block below to each language version of the site
(`/privacy` = TR, `/privacy/en`, `/privacy/de`, `/privacy/fr`, `/privacy/es`,
`/privacy/ru`, `/privacy/it`). Keep the heading style consistent with the
existing numbered sections. Update each page's "last updated" date.

Accurate to the shipped app (v1.8.1+35): read-only, opt-in (default off), only
**workout sessions** + their **active-energy (calories)** are read, calories are
display-only (never affect the budget), never shared, never used for ads,
deleted with the account, and Nuveli never writes back to Health Connect.

## 🇬🇧 English

**Health Data Connection — Health Connect (Android)**

When you enable "Connect phone health data" in Settings (off by default),
Nuveli reads only your workout sessions from Android Health Connect: activity
type, start time, duration, and the session's active energy (calories). This
data is shown only in your in-app activity log; the calories shown are for
information only and are never added to your daily calorie budget. Access is
read-only — we never write to Health Connect, never share this data with third
parties, and never use it for advertising. You can revoke access anytime in
Settings or the Health Connect app. Imported workouts are deleted when you
delete your account.

**Gesundheitsdaten-Verbindung — Health Connect (Android)**

Wenn Sie in den Einstellungen "Telefon-Gesundheitsdaten verbinden" aktivieren
(standardmäßig aus), liest Nuveli ausschließlich Ihre Trainingseinheiten aus
Android Health Connect: Aktivitätstyp, Startzeit, Dauer und die aktive Energie

angezeigt; die angezeigten Kalorien dienen nur zur Information und werden niemals
Ihrem täglichen Kalorienbudget hinzugefügt. Der Zugriff erfolgt nur lesend — wir
schreiben niemals in Health Connect, geben diese Daten niemals an Dritte weiter
und verwenden sie niemals für Werbung. Sie können den Zugriff jederzeit in den
Einstellungen oder in der Health-Connect-App widerrufen. Importierte Workouts
werden gelöscht, wenn Sie Ihr Konto löschen.

## 🇷🇺 Русский

добавляются к вашему дневному бюджету калорий. Доступ только для чтения — мы
никогда не записываем данные в Health Connect, никогда не передаём их третьим
лицам и никогда не используем для рекламы. Вы можете отозвать доступ в любой
момент в Настройках или в приложении Health Connect. Импортированные тренировки
удаляются при удалении вашего аккаунта.
